[Function]
(transpose freq interval [scale])

Returns the transposed value of freq in scale. freq may be a note name, key number or list of the same. Interval may be an integer, encoded interval or list of the same. 

cm> (transpose 'a4 1)
as4
cm> (transpose 69 -1)
68
cm> (transpose '(c4 e g) (interval 'aug 4))
(fs4 as4 cs5)
cm> (note (transpose '(60 61) '(0 4 7) ))
((c4 e4 g4) (cs4 f4 af4))
cm> (note (transpose '(0 4 7) '(60 61)))
((c4 cs4) (e4 f4) (g4 af4))
cm>
