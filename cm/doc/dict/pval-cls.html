[Class]
pval

Blocks the evaluation of expr until next encounters it in a pattern. The name pval stands for "pattern value".

Examples:

Example 1. Embed a random number selection as the second element of a pattern. 

(define x (new cycle :of (list 'a 
                               (pval (between 1000 5000))
                               'b)))
(next x 12)
(a 2760 b a 3589 b a 1175 b a 1515 b)
