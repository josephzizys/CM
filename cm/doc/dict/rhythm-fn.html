[Function]
(rhythm [tempo] [beat])

Returns the value of rhythm converted to seconds. Rhythm can be a proportion (number), a rhythmic symbol or list of the same. Tempo is an optional metronome speed, and defaults to *tempo*. The optional Beat parameter specifies the metronome's pulse and defaults to *beat*.

A rhythmic symbol consists of a metric letter (Table 1) that may be optionally preceded by a division letter: t for triplet or q for quintuplet, and optionally followed by any number of dots. Rhythmic symbols can be combined into simple expressions involving addition, subtraction and multiplication.

Table 1. Metric letters.
